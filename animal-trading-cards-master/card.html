Hamster

The most interesting fact I like about Hamsters is That they love to get out of their habitat to enjoy playing.

  • Scientific Name: Cricetinae.
  • Average Length: 4.5 - 5 cm.
  • Average Lifespan: 3 years
  • Habitat: steppes, sand dunes and the edges of deserts.

Hamsters belong to the Cricetinae subfamily which contains almost 25 species. Hamsters are very popular among people as small house pets. Hamsters get to feed on seeds, vegetables, and fruits.Hamsters have large cheek and short tail.
